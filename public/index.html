    instructions

    Objective:


    • You play as Noah
    • Your goal is to bring as many animals as you can to the arc before the flood occurs and the timer runs out.
    • You must compete with other players who also play as Noah to see who can save the most animals.
    • You can steal animals that other players are holding.
    • The player with the most animals saved once the flood occurs wins!

    Controls:


    • Use the w, a, s, d keys to move around the map(jump, left, drop, right).
    • Press the space bar while touching an animal to pick it up.
